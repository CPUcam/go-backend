How Does TruRecruit Work?

TruRecruit makes large scale recruiting easy and painless.

  • Scan in resumés

    Painlessly scan in a resumé using a candidate's unique QR code.

  • Manage Resumés

    Once scanned, resumés appear instantly on any connected laptop or tablet, and are immediately made available to download.

  • Export Data

    Be able to export candidate resumé and data in a variety of formats.
